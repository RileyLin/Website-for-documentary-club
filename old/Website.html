Damien High School Documentary Club

Damien High School Documentry Club is established in September in 2015 by Aiden Song

Who we are? We are:

a group of students who dedicated in presenting the world what the life of Chinese students in America. We come to another country thousands miles away from home to experience another form of life and study. We try to record our life and feelings as a mediator to display the life of young high school international students.


We have fun:

We like to experience the American culture and we recorded where we have been. This is a video recording our happy Sunday in Santa Monica beach. Everyone had fun and enjoyed the American life style. We realized that there are so many beautiful places in this country for us to explore and experience.We like the beach!!


We get together when we are away from home

We like our host parents, they care about us and we are being best taken care of. This video was taken in one of our host parents' house. We had a party there and everyone had fun in the pool.We really appreciate the efforts of our host parents. Thank you!


We also record other people's life

This is a video of one of our host parents' wedding. We are really happy to be a part of it and helped them record one of their most important moments in their life. Again, we thank all those host parents who pay their best efforts to take care of our international students who are alone in this country and give us the feeling of home.
